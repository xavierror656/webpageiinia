# Introduction to Industrial AI

**Industrial artificial intelligence** is revolutionizing the way manufacturing companies operate in the 21st century. From process optimization to predictive maintenance, AI offers unprecedented opportunities to improve efficiency and profitability.

## What is Industrial AI?

Industrial AI refers to the application of machine learning, computer vision, and predictive analytics technologies in manufacturing environments. Unlike consumer AI, industrial AI must:

- Operate in real-time
- Be highly reliable
- Integrate with legacy systems
- Comply with industrial regulations

## Main Use Cases

### 1. Predictive Maintenance

IoT sensors combined with ML algorithms can predict failures before they occur, reducing downtime by up to 70%.

### 2. Quality Control

Computer vision can detect defects imperceptible to the human eye, improving final product quality.

### 3. Process Optimization

Optimization algorithms can adjust production parameters in real-time, maximizing throughput and minimizing waste.

## Conclusion

Industrial AI is not the future - it's the present. Companies that adopt these technologies today will be better positioned to compete tomorrow.
